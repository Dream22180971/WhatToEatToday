Citrus Glazed Summer Salmon

家常菜 快手菜
schedule Time 20 Mins
local_fire_department Calories 340 Kcal
star Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

2 Servings
1
Fresh Salmon
2
Lemon Juice
5g
Olive Oil
10g
Wild Honey
1
Asparagus
pinch
Sea Salt

Cooking Steps

1

Pat the salmon dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with sea salt and cracked black pepper. Preheat your pan over medium-high heat with a drizzle of olive oil.

2

Place the salmon skin-side down in the pan. Sear for 4-5 minutes until the skin is crispy. While searing, whisk together the lemon juice and honey in a small bowl.

3

Flip the salmon and pour the citrus glaze over it. Cook for another 2-3 minutes while spooning the glaze over the fish until it thickens and coats the fillet beautifully.
