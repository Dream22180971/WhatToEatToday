Citrus Glazed Summer Salmon

家常菜 快手菜
schedule Time 20 Mins ⏰
local_fire_department Cals 340 Kcal 🔥
star Rank Easy 👍
🥕

Ingredients

2 Servings
1 Fresh Salmon 🐟
2 Lemon Juice 🍋
5g Olive Oil 🍶
10g Wild Honey 🍯
1 Asparagus 🥦
✨ Sea Salt 🧂
You got this!
👨‍🍳

Let's Cook!

1
🧂

Pat the salmon dry. Season generously with sea salt and black pepper. Preheat pan over medium-high heat.

2
🔥

Place salmon skin-side down. Sear for 4-5 minutes until crispy. Whisk lemon juice and honey.

3
✨

Flip salmon and pour glaze. Cook for 2-3 minutes while spooning glaze over fish until perfectly coated.
